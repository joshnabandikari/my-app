Apollo 11

Armstrong spent about three and a half two and a half hours outside the spacecraft,Aldrin slightly less;and together they they collected 47.5pounds(21.5kg) of lunar material for return to Earth.A third member of the misson,Michael collins,piloted the command spacecraft alone in lunar orbit until Armstrong and Aldrin returned to it for the trip back to Earth
